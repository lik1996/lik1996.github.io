About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in EOS Department of ITC Faculty, University of Twente, supervised by Prof. George Vosselman and Prof. Michael Ying Yang (University Of Bath, UK). My research interests lie in Interactive Segmentation and Visual Question Answering via deep learning based techiniques.


Feel free to contact me if you are interested in the similar topics.
